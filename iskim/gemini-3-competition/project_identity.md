### 1. Impact (40%) - "Solves a Real Problem"

- **Our Angle**: "Gamification for Sustainability".
- **Argument**: Most food waste apps fail because they feel like homework. BitBites works because it's _fun_. We use AI to change human behavior (Behavioral Design).

### 2. Technical Depth (30%) - "Does it utilize Gemini 3?"

- **Our Angle**: "Beyond labelling".
- **Strength**: We don't just ask "What is this?". We ask:
  1.  What is this? (Video Vision)
  2.  What is its 'RPG' archetype? (Creative Reasoning)
  3.  What can I make with it that fits a 'Quest' theme? (Complex Context)
- _Competitor Comparison_: Others just tag images. We transform context.

### 3. Creativity (20%) - "Originality"

- **Our Angle**: "Genre-bending".
- **Strength**: Mixing **Utility** (Pantry Tracker) with **Gaming** (RPG). The 8-bit aesthetic visually differentiates us from the sea of "clean corporate AI dashboards".

### 4. Presentation (10%) - "Wow Factor"

- **Our Angle**: "Nostalgia".
- **Strategy**: The demo video will start with boring reality (grey filter), then "GLITCH" into vibrant 8-bit pixel art when Gemini activates. Sound effects (coin sounds) are crucial.
